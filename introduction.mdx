    <Step title="Set up your company">
      Create your organization and configure company settings, including fiscal information and organizational structure.
      
      Navigate to **Settings** → **Company Management** to add your first company.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Configure authentication">
      Choose your authentication method: traditional email/password or Microsoft SSO integration.
      
      ```bash
      # For Microsoft SSO, configure your environment variables
      MICROSOFT_TENANT_ID=your-tenant-id
      MICROSOFT_ALLOWED_DOMAINS=yourdomain.com
      ```
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Add employees">
      Import or manually add employee records with complete personal, contractual, and payroll information.
      
      Use the bulk import feature to onboard multiple employees at once via CSV upload.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Set up access control">
      Define roles and assign permissions to control what users can view and modify within the system.
      
      <Accordion title="Example role configuration">
      ```json
      {
        "codigo_rol": "PAYROLL_ADMIN",
        "nombre_rol": "Payroll Administrator",
        "descripcion_rol": "Full access to payroll operations",
        "permissions": ["payroll:view", "payroll:edit", "payroll:process"]
      }
      ```
      </Accordion>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
